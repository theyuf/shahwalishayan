CovEntry

The app CovEntry was designed for Coventry city council keeping their needs and the needs of the public in mind. The main idea was to use a coherrent color scheme and elements in alignment with Coventry city council. 

The software used for wireframing the app included Sketch. Adobe Creative Suite was used to further visualise the wireframes and to come up with visual elements of the prototype.

Around 27 screens were designed and then each element was linked to create a seamless UI/UX experience.

The app design was then finally animated to create a working prototype with the help of InVision.

DOPEaMEAN

DOPEaMEAN is streetwear brand and the pre-launch website can be seen below.

The whole idea of creating this experience was to keep everything consistent with the brand's aesthetic. The website is very minimal and uses a GIF in the background to help it load easily when opened across different devices.

The GIF shows a series of images that portray the 'Process of Creation'. The parallex bit of the website shows some of the digital art creations used by the brand on their instagram.

The website was created to maintain a database of subscribers prior to the launch and to guage the potential of the brand through it's pre-hype marketing campaings.
